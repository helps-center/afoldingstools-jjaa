Privacy Policy

**1. About Your Privacy**

Your privacy matters a lot to us. In this Privacy Policy, we let you know how we collect, use, and keep your personal info safe when you visit, sign up for, or use our news website.

**2. What Info We Grab**

We might collect:

*   **Account &amp; Sign-Up Stuff:**
    *   Your name, email, and a password.
    *   Details about your sign-up, and how you pay (it's all handled securely by other companies).
    *   If you want our newsletter.
*   **What You Do on the Site:**
    *   What you say in comments, how you rate stuff.
    *   Likes, shares, and other things you click on.
*   **Techy Stuff:**
    *   Your IP address, what kind of browser you're using, your device type.
    *   Which pages you look at, how long you stay, and what you click.
*   **Extra Info (If You Want to Share):**
    *   Stuff you send in surveys, contests, or just general feedback.

**3.What We Do with Your Info**

We use your info to:

*   Keep your sign-up going and give you the content.
*   Answer your comments, questions, and all that.
*   Send you newsletters and updates by email (if you ask for them).
*   Suggest content you might like.
*   See how people use the site so we can make it better.
*   Stop bad stuff like fraud and spam.

**4. Other Services We Use**

We might share info with:

*   Companies that process payments (like Stripe, PayPal).
*   Companies that host our site and data (like AWS, Google Cloud).
*   Companies that give us analytics (like Google Analytics).
*   Companies that send our emails (like Mailchimp).

These services only get the info they absolutely need, and they have to follow privacy rules too.

**5. Cookies and Tracking**

We use cookies (small files) to:

*   Keep you logged in.
*   See how you use the website.
*   Show you stuff you might like.
*   Remember what you like for your sign-up.

You can control cookies in your browser's settings.

**6. How Long We Keep Your Data**

We keep your personal and sign-up info as long as your account is open, or if the law says we have to. You can close your account and delete your info whenever you want.

**7. Your Rights**

You can:

*   See what personal info we have about you.
*   Fix or change your info.
*   Close your account and delete your sign-up data.
*   Stop getting promotional emails.
*   Change your cookie and tracking settings.

**8. Keeping Your Data Safe**

We do what we can to protect your personal and payment info. But no system is perfect, so please keep your password safe.

**9. Kids' Privacy**

This site is not for kids under 13. We don't knowingly collect info from them.

**10. Changes to This Policy**

We might change this Privacy Policy sometimes. If we do, we'll post the changes here with a new date.
